Delicious Orange Ginger Glazed Salmon

★★★★

Dinner, Lunch

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: Calories: 306 kcal Source: livingsweetmoments.com

Ingredients

1 teaspoon Fresh Ginger minced

2 cloves Garlic minced

1 tablespoon Sesame Oil

1/4 Cup Honey

2 Oranges juiced

1/4 cup Soy Sauce

Green Onion Sliced

2 lb Salmon

Description

Honey, orange, ginger, soy sauce glazed salmon. Serve on brown rice or quinoia.

Directions

Mix the ginger, garlic, sesame oil, honey, orange juice, soy sauce and green onion in a bowl. Place in a large zip top bag.

Insert the salmon in the zip top bag and let it marinade for about 15 to 30 minutes in the fridge.

Preheat oven to 425 Degrees Fahrenheit

Remove the salmon from the fridge and place it in a rectangular baking pan. Include the marinade.

Insert the fish in the oven and bake for 13 minutes. Remove when the center is pink.

Enjoy!

Notes

Can substitute orange marnelade for OJ
